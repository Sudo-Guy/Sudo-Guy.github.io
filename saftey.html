Safety framework

Safety is a system: decisions, habits, and redundancy. This page is designed to be used as a quick pre-departure check and a shared partner standard.

Non-negotiable: If conditions exceed the group’s comfort/skill level, turning around is success.

Cold injury prevention

  • Stay drySweat management is warmth management. Change early, not late.
  • Hands/feet checksRegularly check numbness, dexterity, and “hot spots.”
  • Stop-layer disciplineInsulate immediately during breaks.

Navigation + exposure risk

  • Treeline rulesWind and visibility change fast; define thresholds before you start.
  • Terrain selectionAvoid committing lines when uncertain; choose low-consequence routes.
  • Turn-around triggersTime, weather, energy, and traction limits.
