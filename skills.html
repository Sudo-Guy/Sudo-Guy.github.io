Skills beat gear

Winter travel rewards discipline: pace, temperature control, navigation, and clear communication. This page is structured so you can add lessons learned, drills, and partner standards.

Pacing & heat management

  • Start coldBegin slightly underdressed; warm up through movement.
  • Micro-adjustSmall changes early prevent sweat-soak problems later.
  • Food + water timingPlan warm stops; reduce “bonk” risk.

Navigation fundamentals

  • Map awarenessKnow bailouts, ridgelines, and exposure zones.
  • RedundancyPhone + offline maps + backup battery; add compass skills.
  • Whiteout habitsShort legs, bearings, “stop if unsure” rule.

Partner systems

  • Shared standardsTurn-around rules, check-ins, and decision triggers.
  • SignalsWind-proof communication plan.
  • Practice daysSkill-building trips before committing to big routes.

Ready-to-fill: drills & milestones

  • Layering drillTime yourself changing layers with gloves on; do it without losing gear.
  • Navigation drillPlan and follow a short bearing-based route in safe terrain.
  • Traction drillSafe practice on low-consequence slopes; learn transitions.
